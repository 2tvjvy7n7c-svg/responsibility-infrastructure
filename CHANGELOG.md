### Central contribution

Version 1.0 introduces the working concept of **responsibility infrastructure** for consequential AI-assisted work.

It distinguishes activity logging from a governed responsibility record and proposes the following formulation:

> Authority enters through a mandate. Evidence and decisions persist through a governed record. Responsibility exits through explicit acceptance.
